Nancy Slay


1948 - 2013

Viewing & Rosary

Friday 10th January, from 3-7pm

A viewing will take place, January 10th at the Chapel of the Valley,
1755 East Avenue R, Palmdale, CA. There will be a reading of the Rosary
by family members and her lifelong friend, Sister Beatrice Marie.


Funeral Service

Saturday 11th January, at 10am

Funeral services for Nancy will be held at Father Serra Parish,
42121 60th Street West, Quartz Hill (Lancaster), CA.

Following the services, Nancy will be buried at Good Shepherd Cemetery (a Catholic Cemetery owned and operated by the Los Angeles Archdiocese),
at 43121 70th Street West, just a few left turns from the church.
